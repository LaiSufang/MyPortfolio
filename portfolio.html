UC Crime Map

April - August 2024

UC Crime Map is a prototype I helped design to improve safety awareness at the University of Cincinnati. It's an interactive map that shows real-time crime data and hotspots, specifically tailored for UC campus. The goal is to make it easier for students, faculty, and staff to stay informed and navigate safely on UC campus.


A-Maze-ing-Space

April 2024

A-Maze-ing-Space is a 2D video game I co-designed to explore how individuals navigate virtual environments when provided with different types of route information. The game challenges players to find their way through intricate mazes while collecting data on their decision-making and spatial awareness. This project merges gaming with research, offering insights into cognitive mapping and navigation strategies in digital spaces.
